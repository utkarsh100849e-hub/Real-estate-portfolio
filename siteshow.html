4BHK • 3500 sqft • Prime Location

Welcome to 21 Marina Blvd, a private yet community-forward home in full awareness of its place perched atop the Marina Highlands.

Perched above a desirable enclave in the Marina Highlands neighborhood, sits a special 1940s split-level property with ever-changing and expansive views, vintage character, and midcentury modern appeal. Bathed in all-day sunlight and framed by tree-lined streets, it’s the kind of home where morning light is cast into sunny corners, where bedroom windows frame canyon views, and where quiet afternoons are spent in awareness of the home and its surroundings. The heart of this home is its open and flexible living spaces, lined with picture windows and anchored by a clay-tiled fireplace. Two bedrooms and one-and-a-half bathrooms offer comfort and convenience, with period details lovingly preserved throughout. A Merrill & O’Keefe vintage stove, original casement windows, and black-and-white tile flooring in the kitchen nod to the home’s heritage, while the integrative layout provides a spacious and easy flow throughout. Multiple outdoor spaces on the street-to-street lot offer a space to feel centered and creative, with plenty of room for gardening, play, and entertaining. A separate, spacious, multi-car garage and workshop, along with basement storage and laundry, add utility and flexibility. The neighborhood offers both privacy and connection, where kids learn to ride bikes on the street, where neighbors are out walking their dogs, and where minutes away you’ll find downtown San Rafael, the Marin Yacht Club, Loch Lomond Marina, McNears Beach, the trails at China Camp, and neighborhood favorites like Andy’s Market. Lifestyle, community and convenience - the best of all worlds.
